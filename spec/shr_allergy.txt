//   Risk Posed by a Substance, including allergies and intolerances

Entry:			SubstanceRisk
Concept:		TBD
Description:	"A postive or negative clinical statement pertaining to an individual's allergies and intolerances to substances or classes of substances."
1..1			(PositiveSubstanceRisk or NegativeSubstanceRisk)

	Element:		PositiveSubstanceRisk
	Concept:		TBD
	Description:	"A record of a clinical assessment of an allergy or intolerance; a propensity, or a potential risk to an individual, to have an adverse reaction on future exposure to the specified substance, or class of substance."
	1..1			(PositiveSubstanceRiskAssertion or PostiveSubstanceRiskSubstance)
	0..*			UsualManifestation
	0..1			Criticality
	0..1			Onset    // from core

		Element:		PositiveSubstanceRiskAssertion
		Concept:		TBD
		Description:	"A coded assertion of an allergy or intolerance that includes the substance in the code."
		1..1			code descending from SCT #420134006 [Propensity to adverse reactions]

		Element:		PostiveSubstanceRiskSubstance
		Concept:		TBD
		Description:	"A substance that poses a risk of adverse reaction."
		1..1 			Substance

		Element:		UsualManifestation
		Concept:		TBD
		Description:	"The expected or usual symptoms that result from the exposure, such as sneezing, rash, etc."
		1..1			Symptom // from core

		Element:		Criticality
		Concept:		MTH #C3858539 [Criticality]
		Description:	"A clinical judgment as to the worst case result of a future exposure (including substance administration). When the worst case result is assessed to have a life-threatening or organ system threatening potential, it is considered to be of high criticality."
		1..1			code from http://hl7.org/fhir/allergy-intolerance-criticality



	Element:		NegativeSubstanceRisk
	Concept:		TBD
	Description:	"A statement of no known association of a substance and risk of adverse reaction"
	1..1			(NegativeSubstanceRiskAssertion or NegativeSubstanceRiskSubstance)

		Element:		NegativeSubstanceRiskAssertion
		Concept:		TBD
		Description:	"A coded assertion of no known adverse reaction risk that includes the substance or substance class in the code."
		1..1			code descending from SCT #716186003 [No known allergy]

		Element:		NegativeSubstanceRiskSubstance
		Concept:		TBD
		Description:	"A substance that poses no known risk for an adverse reaction."
		1..1 			Substance

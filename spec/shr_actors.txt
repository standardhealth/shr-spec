DataDefinitions:	shr.actors
Uses:				shr.core, shr.demographics

/*
	People and organizations that are:
	- involved in care
	- related to the person of record (may be alive of dead)
	- anyone who may be contacted(such as a case manager)

	Roles record that a person is acting in a certain capacity with respect to the person of record during a period of time
*/

// Just the people... not their role in care...
